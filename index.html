The Problem

Most outbound fails because it shouldn't have been sent.

Traditional lead generation optimizes for volume. More contacts, more emails, more sends. But inbox providers got smarter, buyers got more skeptical, and reply rates collapsed. The answer isn't sending more. It's knowing when not to send at all.

Traditional Lead Gen

Volume-first outbound

  • Buy lists of contacts, hope some are relevant
  • Blast campaigns to everyone who fits basic criteria
  • Measure success by sends and opens
  • Burn domains trying to scale past limits
  • No visibility into actual buying readiness

  • Know why an account is ready before you reach out
